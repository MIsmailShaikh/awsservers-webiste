Refunds & Cancellations

First-time customers can request a refund within 7 days if the server is not provisioned or is unusable due to our fault.

No refunds for misuse, violation of policies, or heavy usage of server resources.

Service is billed monthly; customers must request cancellation before the next renewal date to stop future charges.

Refunds, where applicable, are processed back to the original payment method via the payment gateway.
